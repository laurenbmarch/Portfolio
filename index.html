About Me

Hello! My name is Lauren March. I am a Catholic, wife, mother to three boys, musician, and aspiring web and software developer.

I started off on my career journey as a 1st grade teacher. I loved using my creativity to assist my students in learning about the world around them.

After getting married and becoming a mother, I decided to work for the Catholic Church as a pastoral musician. I loved using my gift of singing to bring beauty to the most important moments of people's lives: baby's baptisms, First Holy Communions, Graduations, Weddings, and Funerals.

I currently work as the Director of Liturgical Music for a Catholic Church in Fishers, Indiana.

I love meeting new people and collaborating creativity to make our world a better place.
